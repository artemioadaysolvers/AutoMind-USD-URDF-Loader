AutoMind MJCF Viewer BUILD212

Fixes
-----
1. Keeps the original joint-axis color and all MJCF kinematics unchanged.

2. Before showing a cyan joint axis, validates that the animated pivot is supported by
   the direct visual geometry of both the parent and child links. An unsupported
   CAD-body origin is suppressed only as a viewport decoration.

3. This removes contextless/ghost axes such as the stray cyan segment that can appear
   inside a gripper finger when a nested body carries an offset visual mesh.

4. The test runs in the current kinematic frames, so markers stay synchronized while
   the mechanism, loops and downstream bodies move.

No MJCF XML, joint, loop, coupling, material, colour, selection or solver data is modified.
