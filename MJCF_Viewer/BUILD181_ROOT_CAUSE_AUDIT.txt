BUILD181 root-cause audit

Observed mismatch
- USD/URDF+ currentMatrix is computed after the joint motion transform.
- MJCF used bodyPose.matrixWorld as currentMatrix.
- In MJCF parseBody, bodyPose owns the static body pose; moving meshes and children are added under content = pivot -> motion -> inverse.
- Therefore bodyPose did not change when its own joint changed.
- Loop frames, closure residuals, and orange loop lines were evaluated at q=0 while render geometry moved.

Fix
- Each MJCF link now retains bodyPose as its render/selection root.
- Each linkInfo.group now points to content after all scalar joint transforms.
- _refreshLinkMatrices now receives the same moving body frame that is seen on screen.

Expected verification
- At a nonzero joint angle, currentMatrix for link_13_Gripper_1/link_14_Gripper_2/link_15_Link_1/link_16_Link_2 must change.
- Orange P8/P5 segments should collapse near zero after closure solve instead of remaining at the q=0 reference pose.
